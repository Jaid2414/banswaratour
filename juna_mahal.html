Juna Mahal is a historic fort in Dungarpur, built in the 13th century. Located on a hill, it is known for its intricate paintings and murals inside the palace. Juna Mahal was once the royal residence of the Dungarpur kingdom.

How to Reach

Juna Mahal is located around 3 km from the center of Dungarpur city. It can be easily accessed by taxi or personal vehicle.
