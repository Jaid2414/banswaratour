Anand Sagar Lake is a popular tourist attraction in Banswara, Rajasthan. Also known as 'Bai Talab', it features beautiful cenotaphs and towers around the lake.

How to Reach

The lake is 3 km from Banswara city. You can reach by taxi, auto, or private vehicle. Nearest railway station is Ratlam; nearest airport is Udaipur.
